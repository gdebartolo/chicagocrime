Social Infrastructure and Crime in Chicago

The following map examines spatial trends of social infrastructure and crime in Chicago communities. The factors that can be explored are: violent crimes per 10,000 people (vcrimes_pt), libraries per 10,000 people (lib_ptt), parks per 10,000 people (parks_ptt), grocery stores per 10,000 people (groc_ptt), worship centers per 10,000 people (worship_pt), and daycare centers per 10,000 people (elc_ptt) .
